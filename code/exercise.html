Running

Running primarily engages the lower body musdes,
incuding quadriceps, hamstrings, calves, and glutes. It's an excellent cardiovascular exercise that strengthens the heart and improves overall endurance. Running also engages the core muscles for stability and balance.

Barbell Curls

Barbell curls specifically target the bicep muscles, located on the front of the upper arm. This exercise helps in building bicep muscle mass and strength. It also engages the forearm muscles to a certain extent.

Barbell shoulder push

The barbell shoulder push, or shoulder press, primarily targets the deltoid muscles of the shoulders. It also engages the triceps as secondary musces. Additionally, this exercise activates the upper back muscles and the core for stability during the movement.
