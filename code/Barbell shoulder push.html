Barbell shoulder push

1. Push: mainly practice the anterior, middle and posterior deltoid muscles Action: Many people will ignore the shoulder practice, in fact, whether it is men or women should practice the shoulder, the first is sitting, two hand dumbbells on the side of the body, two elbow abduction, palm facing forward, push the dumbbells to the highest point in an arc, stop slightly, slowly control the dumbbells according to the original route (arc) reduction. Tip: It can also be done in a standing position, with both arms at the same time, or with one arm rotation.

2. Side horizontal lift: mainly practice the middle tract of deltoid muscle Action: Two hand-held dumbbells hang in front of the legs, lean forward slightly, bend the elbows slightly, lift the dumbbells to both sides to shoulder height, so that the deltoid muscle is in the "peak contraction" position, pause a little, and then the shoulder muscle control slowly returns. You can do it with one arm, two arms.

3. Lean sideways horizontal lift: mainly practice the deltoid muscle posterior bundle Action: Two hand dumbbells, palms facing each other, bent over knees, body stability, two arms to the sides, and then control slow recovery.

4. Shrug: Mainly engage the trapezius muscle Action: Two hand-held dumbbells hanging on the side of the body, knees slightly bent, upper body slightly forward, shoulders fully raised, try to touch the acromion earlobe, pause a little, and then slowly control the recovery.
